Q2: Longest consecutive sequence from an un-ordered array.
Rules:
    1. seq will have + integers including 0
    2. you can flip 0's to any required number in order to make a seq
    3. return length of longest consecutive seq

e.g.

INPUT:

0 6 5 0 3 10 11

OUTPUT

5

EXPLAINATION:

0 => 7
6
5
0 => 4
3
